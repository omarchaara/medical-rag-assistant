Heart Disease Prevention
Cardiovascular disease refers to conditions that involve narrowed or blocked blood vessels that can lead to heart attack, chest pain (angina), or stroke.

Risk Factors:
- High blood pressure
- High cholesterol
- Smoking
- Diabetes
- Obesity
- Family history
- Age

Prevention Strategies:
- Regular aerobic exercise
- Heart-healthy diet (Mediterranean)
- Smoking cessation
- Weight management
- Stress reduction
- Regular health screenings
